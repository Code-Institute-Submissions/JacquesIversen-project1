Moving into 2022

Coming into the seventh season of his F1 career, Max Verstappen drove some exceptional races to eventually win the world championship. On the very final lap of the Abu Dhabi Grand Prix, he denied his title rival Lewis Hamilton a record-breaking eighth world championship title. While Verstappen was expected to be the first since 2016 to truly challenge Hamilton for the title, not many expected victory would come this early for him. The Red Bull driver ended the 2020 season 133 points behind Hamilton, securing a P3 in the drivers' standings. Considering such facts, to have won the championship a year later by an eight-point lead at the young age of 24, is extraordinary.
